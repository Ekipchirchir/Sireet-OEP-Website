ABOUT US

Sireet OEP, By farmers for Farmers

Sireet Outgrowers Empowerment Project Company Limited (Sireet OEP) is a Fairtrade certified tea producer based in Nandi Hills, Kenya, specializing in providing high-quality, disease-resistant seedlings to help you establish a thriving farm.

“We partner with farmers by providing expert advice and reliable varieties tailored to your climate and soil.”

Frequently Asked Questions

How does Sireet OEP support farmers?

We provide training, fair market access, sustainable practices, artificial insemination services, farm inputs, and bursaries to 6,235 farmers (with some records indicating up to 8,017 members).

What is the tea production capacity?

Our factory processes 98,000 kg of green leaf daily, supplying tea to six EPK factories: Kapsumbeiwa, Kepchomo, Chemomi, Savani, Siret, and Kibwari.

How can I get involved?

Contact us to explore partnership, volunteer opportunities, or visit our guest house and job opportunities sections.
